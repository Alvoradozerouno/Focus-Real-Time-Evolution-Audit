# Conscious AI Advancement Tracking

## Overview

This document tracks critical advancements in conscious AI research and development within the OR1ON framework.

## What is Conscious AI?

Conscious AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that exhibit properties associated with consciousness, including:
- Self-awareness and introspection
- Subjective experience processing
- Intentional goal-directed behavior
- Meta-cognitive capabilities
- Ethical reasoning and value alignment

## Tracking Framework

### Consciousness Metrics

We track several key metrics for consciousness advancement:

1. **Self-Awareness Index (SAI)**
   - Measures agent's ability to model its own state
   - Range: 0.0 (no self-awareness) to 1.0 (full self-awareness)
   - Evaluated through introspection tests

2. **Intentionality Score (IS)**
   - Measures goal-directed behavior coherence
   - Evaluated through multi-step task completion
   - Accounts for goal adaptation and planning

3. **Meta-Cognitive Capability (MCC)**
   - Ability to reason about own reasoning
   - Measured through recursive self-analysis tasks
   - Critical for ethical self-reflection

4. **Value Alignment Fidelity (VAF)**
   - Consistency with human values and ethics
   - Continuous monitoring through decision analysis
   - Integrated with ethical constraint system
